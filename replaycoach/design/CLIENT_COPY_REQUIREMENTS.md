Frequently asked questions
Is the platform only for professional dancers?
No. It can support students, emerging dancers, professional performers, instructors and
choreographers.
Which dance styles can be reviewed?
The platform is designed around body movement rather than one specific dance style, making it
suitable for different forms of dance and movement practice.
Does it replace a dance instructor?
No. It gives instructors and dancers a clearer way to review, communicate and revisit feedback.
Can I compare multiple performances?
Where comparison is enabled, different takes can be reviewed together to identify changes in
timing, positioning and execution.
Can an instructor review a session remotely?
Live or shared review tools can allow dancers and instructors to examine the same performance
without being in the same studio.
Is special equipment required?
Use the actual technical answer here. Do not state that only a phone is required unless that
workflow has been tested.
Is my footage private?
This section should clearly explain video storage, sharing permissions, deletion and access
controls before launch.
