The signature: neon skeleton

Thin (1.5–2px) indigo→violet gradient strokes, soft violet glow, small filled joint dots. No flat per-limb colors. This drawing style is the one signature — it appears on the landing hero, the live overlay in the room, and informs the neon-line language of the analytics charts.
